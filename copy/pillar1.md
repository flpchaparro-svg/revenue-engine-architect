## Tiers & personas (from constants/pillar1Data.ts)

### STARTER — "I need a website that actually works."
- Summary: `Choose this if you're a tradie or local service business. You need a website that captures enquiries while you're on the tools.`
- Sprint: `7 DAYS`
- Specs: Ready in 7 days | Built on WordPress | Works perfectly on phones | Shows up on Google
- **The After-Hours Tradie** (Emergency plumbers, locksmiths, after-hours electricians) — Pain: The Missed Call — `You're under a sink at 11pm. Phone rings. You can't answer. That job goes to the next bloke on Google.` — Fix: `I set up instant SMS replies. When you miss a call, they get a text saying you'll call back within the hour. The job stays yours.`
- **The Quote Runner** (Pest control, solar installers, cleaners, pool guys) — Pain: The Wasted Quote — `You drive 45 minutes to quote a job. Turns out their budget is $200 for a $2,000 job. You just wasted half a day on a tyre-kicker.` — Fix: `I build forms that ask the right questions before they book. Budget, job size, timeline. You only quote jobs worth quoting.`
- **The Local Expert** (NDIS providers, physios, accountants, lawyers) — Pain: The Free Advice Trap — `You spend 10 hours a week on calls with people who can't afford you or don't actually need you.` — Fix: `I build intake forms that filter out the time-wasters before they reach your phone.`

### E-COMMERCE — "I sell products online."
- Summary: `Choose this if you sell physical products online. You need a store that syncs with your stock and calculates shipping properly.`
- Sprint: `14 DAYS`
- Specs: Built on Shopify | Syncs with your till system | Calculates shipping automatically | Sends order updates automatically
- **The Boutique Owner** — Pain: The Oversell — Fix: I connect your shop floor to your website...
- **The Heavy Goods Seller** — Pain: The Shipping Problem — Fix: I set up shipping that calculates based on weight, size, and postcode...
- **The Subscription Box** — Pain: The Failed Payment — Fix: I set up automatic payment retries and reminder emails...

### PROFESSIONAL — "I sell high-ticket services."
- Summary: `Choose this if you sell expensive services and your website needs to match. Slow or dated sites cost you deals before you even get the call.`
- Sprint: `21 DAYS`
- Specs: Custom-built, not a template | Loads in under a second | Bank-level security | Connects to your CRM
- **The High-End Builder** — Pain: The Slow Portfolio — Fix: I build a site that loads your high-res photos instantly...
- **The Prestige Seller** — Pain: The Trust Killer — Fix: I build sites with no database to hack...
- **The Authority Site** — Pain: The Google Problem — Fix: I rebuild your site so it loads fast...

### FLAGSHIP — "I need something special."
- Summary: `Choose this if a normal website isn't enough. You need something that makes people stop and stare.`
- Sprint: `30+ DAYS`
- Specs: 3D and animation | Cinematic feel | Custom experience for your customers | Built to impress
- **The Luxury Brand** — Pain: The Template Problem — Fix: I build a site that feels like an experience...
- **The Customiser** — Pain: The Imagination Gap — Fix: I build a configurator where they pick the fabric, the legs, the size...
- **The Boutique Hotelier** — Pain: The Booking.com Tax — Fix: I build a booking experience so smooth and beautiful that guests want to book direct...
